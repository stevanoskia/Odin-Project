Neapolitan style pizza

Description

a classic neapolitan pizza that is made with a few high quality ingredients that makes them stand out

  • 00 flour
  • san marzano canned tomatoes
  • fresh basil
  • buffalo mozarella
  • yeast

Steps:

  1. put some olive oil in a pan and fry up a couple of garlic cloves
  2. add the canned tomatoes, crush them by hand and let them reduce in a nice and thick marinara
  3. for the dough we need to prepare it a day in advance so mix in the flour the yeast and some water until you reach the desired consistency and let it ferment
  4. kneed the dough in a round shape using your knuckles
  5. put your toppings on and put it in a prefferably pizza oven for 1 to 2 minutes or for those cooking in a home oven try to get a pizza stone to heat up to get that bottom char on the pizza and cook for 4 minutes before rotating and waiting another 4 minutes before pulling it out
